## Table 10: Validation Checklist

| Step | Action | Success Criteria |
|------|--------|------------------|
| 1 | List 3-5 business ideas | Clear problem + solution |
| 2 | Identify target audience | Specific person, not "everyone" |
| 3 | Find 10 people in target | Can be online or offline |
| 4 | Ask "Would you pay?" | Not "Do you like it?" |
| 5 | Ask "How much?" | Get specific price point |
| 6 | Count Yes responses | Need 7+/10 to proceed |
| 7 | Get pre-orders | Best validation = actual money |
| 8 | Build MVP | Minimum viable product |
| 9 | Iterate based on feedback | Continuous improvement |
| 10 | Scale what works | Double down on winners |
