## Table 8: Key Metrics

| Metric | Formula | Good | Excellent |
|--------|---------|------|-----------|
| CPM | Cost / (Impressions / 1000) | < $15 | < $10 |
| CPC | Cost / Clicks | < $1 | < $0.50 |
| CTR | (Clicks / Impressions) × 100 | > 1% | > 2% |
| CPA | Cost / Conversions | < Profit margin | < 50% margin |
| ROAS | Revenue / Ad Spend | > 2x | > 4x |
| Frequency | Impressions / Reach | < 3 | < 2 |
| Relevance Score | Facebook rating | > 5 | > 8 |

## Table 12: Common Mistakes & Solutions
