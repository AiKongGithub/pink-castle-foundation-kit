## Table 5: Marketing Metrics Benchmarks

| Metric | Definition | Good | Great | Action if Low |
|--------|------------|------|-------|---------------|
| Opt-in Rate | Leads/Visitors × 100 | 20-30% | 30-40% | Improve Headline/Offer |
| Email Open Rate | Opens/Sent × 100 | 20-30% | 30-40% | Better Subject Lines |
| Click-through Rate | Clicks/Opens × 100 | 3-5% | 5-10% | Better CTA/Copy |
| Conversion Rate | Sales/Visitors × 100 | 1-3% | 3-5% | Optimize Landing Page |
| ROAS | Revenue/Ad Spend | 2-3x | 3-5x | Improve Targeting/Offer |
| Cost per Lead | Ad Spend/Leads | < $5 | < $2 | Better Ads/Targeting |
