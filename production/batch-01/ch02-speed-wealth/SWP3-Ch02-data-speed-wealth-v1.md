## Table 8: Wealth Formula Components

| Component | Definition | How to Improve |
|-----------|------------|----------------|
| Traffic | Number of people entering funnel | More ads, content, channels |
| Conversion | % of visitors who buy | Better copy, offers, targeting |
| Price | Average order value | Upsells, premium tiers, bundles |
| Frequency | How often customers buy again | Recurring products, retention |

**Formula:** Wealth = Traffic × Conversion × Price × Frequency
