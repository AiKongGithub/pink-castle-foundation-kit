## Executive Summary

---

## 1. Content Marketing vs Paid Ads

### 1.1 Comparison

| Aspect | Paid Ads | Content Marketing |
|--------|----------|-------------------|
| Cost over time | Constant/Increasing | Decreasing |
| Traffic when stopped | Stops | Continues |
| Trust | Low initially | High from start |
| Scalability | Limited by budget | Unlimited |
| Long-term ROI | Low | Very High |

### 1.2 Compound Effect

```
Year 1: 52 content pieces
Year 2: 104 content pieces (cumulative)
Year 3: 156 content pieces
...
Traffic compounds as content ranks and gets shared
```

## 2. Funnel Stages

### 2.1 ATTRACT

**Goal:** ดึง traffic เข้ามา

| Channel | Content Type | SEO Focus |
|---------|--------------|-----------|
| Blog | How-to, Lists | Long-tail keywords |
| YouTube | Tutorials | Video SEO |
| Social | Tips, Quotes | Hashtags |
| Podcast | Interviews | Show notes |

### 2.2 ENGAGE

**Goal:** เปลี่ยน visitor เป็น lead

| Lead Magnet | Best For |
|-------------|----------|
| Ebook | In-depth topics |
| Checklist | Action-oriented |
| Template | Tool-based |
| Free course | Education |
| Quiz | Interactive |

### 2.3 NURTURE

**Goal:** สร้าง relationship และ trust

| Email Type | Frequency |
|------------|-----------|
| Welcome | Day 0 |
| Value | Days 1-7 |
| Story | Day 10 |
| Case study | Day 14 |
| Soft offer | Day 21 |

### 2.4 CONVERT

**Goal:** เปลี่ยน lead เป็น customer

| BOFU Content | Purpose |
|--------------|---------|
| Demo | Show product |
| Free trial | Reduce risk |
| Case study | Social proof |
| Consultation | Answer questions |

## 4. Metrics & KPIs

### 4.1 By Funnel Stage

| Stage | Metrics |
|-------|---------|
| Attract | Traffic, Impressions, Rankings |
| Engage | Opt-in rate, Lead magnet downloads |
| Nurture | Open rate, Click rate, Engagement |
| Convert | Conversion rate, Revenue, LTV |

### 4.2 Benchmarks

| Metric | Poor | Average | Good |
|--------|------|---------|------|
| Blog traffic (monthly) | <1K | 5K | 20K+ |
| Opt-in rate | <1% | 2-3% | 5%+ |
| Email open rate | <15% | 25% | 40%+ |
| Content-to-sale | <1% | 1-2% | 3%+ |
