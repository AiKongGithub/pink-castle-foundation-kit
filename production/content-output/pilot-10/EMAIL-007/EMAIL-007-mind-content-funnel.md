## Branch Details

### 1. Funnel Stages

- **Attract**
  - ดึง traffic ด้วย SEO content
  - Social media distribution
  - Answer questions people ask

- **Engage**
  - เปลี่ยน visitor เป็น lead
  - Lead magnet (Ebook, Checklist)
  - Opt-in form

- **Nurture**
  - Email sequence
  - ให้ value ต่อเนื่อง
  - Build relationship

- **Convert**
  - Present offer
  - Match content to need
  - Clear CTA

### 2. Content Types by Stage

- **TOFU (Top of Funnel)**
  - Blog posts
  - Social media posts
  - YouTube videos
  - Infographics
  - Podcast episodes

- **MOFU (Middle of Funnel)**
  - Ebooks
  - Webinars
  - Email courses
  - Case studies
  - Comparisons

- **BOFU (Bottom of Funnel)**
  - Product demos
  - Free trials
  - Consultations
  - Testimonials
  - ROI calculators

- **Repurpose**
  - Blog → Video → Social
  - Webinar → Blog series
  - Podcast → Quote graphics

### 3. Key Metrics

- **Traffic**
  - Organic visits
  - Time on page
  - Pages per session

- **Leads**
  - Opt-in rate
  - Lead quality
  - Cost per lead

- **Sales**
  - Conversion rate
  - Customer lifetime value
  - Attribution

- **ROI**
  - Revenue from content
  - Cost of content
  - Long-term value
