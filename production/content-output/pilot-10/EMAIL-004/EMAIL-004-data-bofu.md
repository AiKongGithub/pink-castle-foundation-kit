## Table 1: Marketing Funnel Stages

| Stage | Full Name | Focus | Content Types | Conversion |
|-------|-----------|-------|---------------|------------|
| TOFU | Top of Funnel | Awareness | Blog, Social, Ads, Video | 100% → 20-30% |
| MOFU | Middle of Funnel | Consideration | Webinar, Email, Ebook | 20-30% → 5-10% |
| BOFU | Bottom of Funnel | Decision | Case Study, Demo, Call | 5-10% → 1-3% |

## Table 5: Guarantee Types

| Guarantee Type | Description | Risk Level | Best For |
|----------------|-------------|------------|----------|
| Money-back | คืนเงินเต็มจำนวน | Low | Products, Courses |
| Partial refund | คืนเงินบางส่วน | Medium | Services |
| Results-based | คืนถ้าไม่ได้ผลลัพธ์ | High | Consulting |
| Free trial | ใช้ฟรีก่อนจ่าย | Low | SaaS, Software |

## Table 6: Urgency Tactics Reference

| Tactic | Example | Duration | Best For |
|--------|---------|----------|----------|
| Hard deadline | "หมดเขต 30 ม.ค. 23:59" | 1-7 days | Launch, Promo |
| Soft deadline | "ราคานี้ถึงสิ้นเดือน" | 2-4 weeks | Evergreen |
| Quantity limit | "รับ 50 คนแรก" | Until sold | Limited product |
| Price increase | "ราคาขึ้น 1 ก.พ." | 1-2 weeks | Pre-launch |
| Bonus expiry | "รับ bonus ภายในวันนี้" | 1-3 days | Add-on value |
