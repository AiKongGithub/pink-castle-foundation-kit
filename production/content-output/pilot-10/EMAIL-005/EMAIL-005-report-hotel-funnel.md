## 1. Hotel Meeting Overview

### 1.1 What is Hotel Meeting?

Hotel Meeting คือการจัด event การขายในห้องประชุมโรงแรม เพื่อนำเสนอสินค้า/บริการราคาสูงแบบ face-to-face

### 1.2 Online vs Offline Comparison

| Aspect | Webinar | Hotel Meeting |
|--------|---------|---------------|
| Location | Online anywhere | Physical venue |
| Trust Level | Medium | High |
| Commitment | Low (easy to skip) | High (travel required) |
| Close Rate | 3-8% | 15-30% |
| Cost | Low | Medium-High |
| Scalability | High | Limited |
| Personal Connection | Low | Very High |

### 1.3 Best For

- High-ticket products (>50,000 THB)
- Complex solutions needing explanation
- Products requiring trust
- VIP/Premium customers

## 3. Event Day Structure

### 3.1 Timeline Template

| Time | Duration | Activity | Goal |
|------|----------|----------|------|
| 0:00 | 15 min | Registration & Welcome | Settle in |
| 0:15 | 15 min | Opening & Hook | Capture attention |
| 0:30 | 45 min | Content & Value | Teach & Build trust |
| 1:15 | 15 min | Case Study/Testimonial | Social proof |
| 1:30 | 20 min | Offer & Bonuses | Present solution |
| 1:50 | 20 min | Q&A & Close | Handle objections |
| 2:10 | 20 min | Order Taking | Collect sales |

### 3.2 Room Setup

| Element | Recommendation |
|---------|----------------|
| Capacity | 20-50 people |
| Seating | Theater or classroom |
| Stage | Elevated if possible |
| AV | Projector + Sound system |
| Decor | Professional, branded |

## 4. Follow-up Strategy

### 4.1 Segment-Based Follow-up

| Segment | Day +1 | Day +3 | Day +7 |
|---------|--------|--------|--------|
| Buyers | Thank you + Onboarding | First value | Check-in |
| Non-buyers | Recap email | Objection handling | Special offer |
| No-shows | Sorry you missed it | Replay/Next event | 1:1 invitation |

### 4.2 Non-Buyer Recovery

```
Day +1: "Thanks for coming, any questions?"
Day +3: "Noticed you're still thinking..."
Day +5: "Extended deadline for attendees"
Day +7: "Last chance + Payment plan"
```
