## Branch Details

### 1. Video Types

- **Short-form**
  - TikTok (15-60 sec)
  - IG Reels (15-90 sec)
  - YouTube Shorts (60 sec)
  - Quick tips, hooks

- **Long-form**
  - YouTube (10-30 min)
  - Webinars (45-90 min)
  - Tutorials
  - Deep content

- **Live**
  - Q&A sessions
  - Behind-the-scenes
  - Product launches
  - Real-time engagement

- **By Funnel Stage**
  - TOFU: Shorts, How-to, Entertainment
  - MOFU: Webinar, Tutorial, Behind-scenes
  - BOFU: Testimonial, Demo, Sales video

### 2. Production Essentials

- **Audio (Priority #1)**
  - Lavalier mic
  - USB condenser
  - Room acoustics
  - Post-processing

- **Lighting**
  - Natural light (window)
  - Ring light
  - Softbox
  - 3-point setup

- **Camera**
  - Smartphone (sufficient)
  - Webcam
  - DSLR/Mirrorless
  - 1080p minimum

- **Editing**
  - Cut dead air
  - Add captions
  - B-roll
  - Music/SFX

### 3. Platforms

- **YouTube**
  - Searchable
  - Long-form home
  - Monetization

- **TikTok**
  - Viral potential
  - Young audience
  - Algorithm discovery

- **Instagram Reels**
  - Existing audience
  - Cross-posting
  - Shopping features

- **Facebook**
  - Older audience
  - Groups
  - Live features
