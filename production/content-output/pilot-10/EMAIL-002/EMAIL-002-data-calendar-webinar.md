## Table 6: Powerpoint Best Practices

| Element | Do | Don't |
|---------|-----|-------|
| Text | 1-3 bullet points | Wall of text |
| Font Size | 24pt minimum | Below 18pt |
| Colors | 2-3 consistent colors | Rainbow colors |
| Images | High quality, relevant | Clip art, stock |
| Slides | 1 idea per slide | Multiple topics |
| Animation | Subtle, purposeful | Excessive, distracting |
