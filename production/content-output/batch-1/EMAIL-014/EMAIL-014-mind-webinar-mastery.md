## Branch Details

### 1. Webinar Structure (80 min total)
- **Hook (5 min):** Big promise, curiosity
- **Story (10 min):** Personal journey
- **Content (30 min):** 3 secrets/steps
- **Transition (5 min):** Bridge to offer
- **Offer (15 min):** Stack presentation
- **Q&A (15 min):** Overcome objections

### 2. Closing Techniques
- **Scarcity:** Limited spots
- **Urgency:** Limited time offer
- **Bonus Stack:** Add value
- **Risk Reversal:** Money-back guarantee

### 3. Technical Setup
- **Platform:** Zoom, Demio, WebinarJam
- **Registration:** Landing page + Email capture
- **Reminders:** 24hr, 1hr, 15min before
- **Replay:** For those who missed
