## Table 2: Perfect Webinar Structure

| Section | Duration | Purpose | Key Elements |
|---------|----------|---------|--------------|
| Hook | 5 min | Grab attention | Promise, curiosity |
| Origin Story | 10 min | Build rapport | Journey, struggles, success |
| Secret 1 | 10 min | Break false belief | Vehicle/opportunity |
| Secret 2 | 10 min | Give method | Internal beliefs |
| Secret 3 | 10 min | Action plan | External beliefs |
| Transition | 5 min | Bridge to offer | "The only thing holding you back..." |
| Stack | 10 min | Show value | Product + bonuses + value |
| Close | 5 min | Call to action | Scarcity, urgency |
| Q&A | 15 min | Handle objections | FAQs, testimonials |

## Table 3: Closing Elements

| Element | Thai | Example | Impact |
|---------|------|---------|--------|
| Scarcity | จำกัดจำนวน | "รับเพียง 20 คน" | Creates FOMO |
| Urgency | จำกัดเวลา | "หมดเขตเที่ยงคืน" | Pushes action |
| Bonus Stack | เพิ่ม bonus | 3-5 bonuses | Increases perceived value |
| Guarantee | รับประกัน | "คืนเงิน 30 วัน" | Reduces risk |
