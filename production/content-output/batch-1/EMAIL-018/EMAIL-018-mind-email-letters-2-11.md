## Branch Details

### 1. Sequence Phases
- **Email 2-3 (Day 2-4):** Value + Education
- **Email 4-5 (Day 5-7):** Stories + Case Studies
- **Email 6-7 (Day 8-10):** Soft Sell + Tease
- **Email 8-9 (Day 11-13):** Hard Sell + Urgency
- **Email 10-11 (Day 14-15):** Last Chance + Close

### 2. Content Types
- **Educational:** Tips, how-to, insights
- **Story-based:** Personal stories, customer journeys
- **Social Proof:** Testimonials, case studies
- **Promotional:** Direct sell, offers

### 3. Best Practices
- **Consistency:** ส่งทุก 1-2 วัน
- **Value First:** ให้ก่อนขาย
- **One CTA:** ต่อ email หนึ่งฉบับ
- **Test & Optimize:** A/B test สม่ำเสมอ
