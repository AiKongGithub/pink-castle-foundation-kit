## Branch Details

### 1. Email Sequences
- **Nurture:** สร้างความสัมพันธ์ (7-14 emails)
- **Sales:** ปิดการขาย (5-7 emails)
- **Re-engage:** ดึงกลับ inactive (3-5 emails)
- **Post-Purchase:** รักษาลูกค้า (5-7 emails)

### 2. Segmentation Types
- **Behavior:** Based on actions taken
- **Interest:** Based on content preferences
- **Engagement:** Active vs Inactive levels
- **Purchase:** Based on buying history

### 3. Automation Triggers
- **Welcome:** When someone subscribes
- **Cart Recovery:** Abandoned shopping cart
- **Birthday:** Annual celebration
- **Win-back:** Long-term inactive
