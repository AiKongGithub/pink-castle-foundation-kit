## Table 3: Production Quality Standards

| Background | Clean, professional | Slightly busy | Distracting |

| Noise Floor | <-60 dB | -50 to -60 dB | >-40 dB |
| Echo/Reverb | None | Minimal | Noticeable |
| Consistency | Even throughout | Minor variation | Uneven |

## Table 4: Framing Standards

### Rule of Thirds Application

| Element | Correct Position | Common Error |
|---------|------------------|--------------|
| Eyes | Upper 1/3 intersection | Dead center |
| Face | Upper 2/3 of frame | Too low or high |
| Headroom | 5-10% of frame | Too much/little |
| Look Space | Space in gaze direction | Cramped |

### Framing Score Guide

| Score | Eyes Position | Headroom | Look Space | Background |

| 7-8 | Near 1/3 | Acceptable | Decent | Minor distraction |
| 5-6 | Off-center | Too much/little | Cramped | Busy |
| 1-4 | Dead center | Very off | None | Very distracting |
