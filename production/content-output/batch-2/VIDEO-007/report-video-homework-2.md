## 3. Script Review Framework

### 3.1 5-Point Script Review System

| Point | Weight | Criteria |
|-------|--------|----------|
| 1. Hook Analysis | 25% | 3-5 sec, Attention-grabbing |
| 2. Structure Check | 25% | Clear AIDA/BAB framework |
| 3. Storytelling | 20% | Personal stories, BAB |
| 4. CTA Evaluation | 15% | Clear, Single focus |
| 5. Language & Tone | 15% | Appropriate, No fillers |

### 3.2 Hook Analysis Criteria

| Element | Excellent | Good | Needs Work | Poor |
|---------|-----------|------|------------|------|
| Timing | <3 sec | 3-5 sec | 5-10 sec | >10 sec |
| Type | Question + Promise | Question OR Promise | Generic statement | "สวัสดีครับ..." |
| Impact | Stop the scroll | Curious | Mild interest | Keep scrolling |
| Score | 10/10 | 7-8/10 | 4-6/10 | 1-3/10 |

### 3.3 Structure Analysis

| Structure Element | Ideal | Common Mistake |
|-------------------|-------|----------------|
| Hook | 5% of video | Too long or missing |
| Introduction | 10% | Too long (>30 sec) |
| Problem Statement | 15% | Too vague |
| Solution/Content | 55% | Unfocused |
| Summary | 10% | Missing |
| CTA | 5% | Too many |

### 3.4 Storytelling Evaluation

| Element | Score if Present | Score if Missing |
|---------|------------------|------------------|
| Personal Story | +4 | 0 |
| Before-After-Bridge | +3 | 0 |
| Specific Examples | +2 | 0 |
| Emotional Connection | +2 | 0 |
| Analogy/Metaphor | +1 | 0 |

## 4. Production Quality Standards

### 4.1 Lighting Quality Matrix

| Factor | Excellent | Acceptable | Poor |
|--------|-----------|------------|------|
| Face Brightness | Even, well-lit | Some shadows | Dark, uneven |
| Background | Clean, appropriate | Slightly busy | Distracting |
| Color Temperature | Consistent | Minor variation | Mixed colors |
| Natural vs Artificial | Balanced | One dominant | Harsh contrast |

### 4.2 Audio Quality Metrics

| Metric | Target | Acceptable | Unacceptable |
|--------|--------|------------|--------------|
| Peak Level | -6 dB | -3 to -12 dB | Clipping or <-15 dB |
| Noise Floor | <-60 dB | -50 to -60 dB | >-40 dB |
| Echo/Reverb | None | Minimal | Noticeable |
| Consistency | Even throughout | Minor variation | Uneven |

### 4.3 Framing Standards

| Element | Correct | Incorrect |
|---------|---------|-----------|
| Eye Position | Upper 1/3 of frame | Center or too high |
| Headroom | 5-10% of frame | Too much or too little |
| Look Space | Space in direction of gaze | Cramped to edge |
| Rule of Thirds | Eyes on intersection | Dead center |

### 4.4 Editing Quality Checklist

| Element | Pass | Fail |
|---------|------|------|
| Dead Air Removed | <0.5 sec gaps | >1 sec gaps |
| Filler Words Cut | 90%+ removed | >5 fillers |
| Transitions | Consistent, minimal | Random, excessive |
| Pacing | Matches content | Too fast or slow |
| Graphics | Readable, timed | Hard to read |

## 5. Common Mistakes Analysis

### 5.1 Mistake Frequency Data

| Mistake | Frequency | Impact Score | Fix Difficulty |
|---------|-----------|--------------|----------------|
| Weak Hook | 82% | Critical (10/10) | Easy |
| Long Introduction | 68% | High (8/10) | Easy |
| No Clear Structure | 55% | High (8/10) | Medium |
| Poor Audio | 45% | Critical (10/10) | Medium |
| Too Many CTAs | 38% | Medium (6/10) | Easy |

### 5.2 Mistake #1: Weak Hook - Detailed Analysis

**Bad Examples Found**:
| Pattern | Frequency |
|---------|-----------|
| "สวัสดีครับ วันนี้ผมจะ..." | 42% |
| No hook, straight to intro | 25% |
| Weak question | 15% |
| Generic statement | 18% |

**Fix Success Rate**:
| Fix Type | Improvement |
|----------|-------------|
| Question Hook | +45% retention |
| Promise Hook | +38% retention |
| Story Hook | +52% retention |
| Shocking Stat | +48% retention |

### 5.3 Mistake #2: Long Introduction

**Time Spent on Introduction**:
| Duration | % of Submissions | Viewer Drop-off |
|----------|------------------|-----------------|
| 0-15 sec | 22% | 8% |
| 16-30 sec | 25% | 15% |
| 31-60 sec | 28% | 28% |
| 61-120 sec | 18% | 45% |
| >120 sec | 7% | 65% |

**Recommendation**: Max 15 seconds for introduction

### 5.4 Mistake #3: No Clear Structure

**Structure Issues Found**:
| Issue | Frequency |
|-------|-----------|
| No visible framework | 35% |
| Jumping between topics | 28% |
| No clear transitions | 22% |
| Missing conclusion | 15% |

### 5.5 Mistake #4: Poor Audio

**Audio Problems Breakdown**:
| Problem | Frequency | Solution |
|---------|-----------|----------|
| Background Noise | 52% | Noise Removal |
| Too Quiet | 28% | Normalize + Boost |
| Echo/Reverb | 15% | Record in smaller room |
| Uneven Volume | 35% | Compressor |
| Muffled Voice | 12% | EQ boost 2-4kHz |

**Impact Data**:
- 60% of viewers close video with poor audio
- Audio quality rated 2x more important than video quality

### 5.6 Mistake #5: Too Many CTAs

**CTA Count Analysis**:
| # of CTAs | Frequency | Conversion Rate |
|-----------|-----------|-----------------|
| 1 | 25% | 4.5% |
| 2 | 30% | 3.2% |
| 3 | 28% | 1.8% |
| 4+ | 17% | 0.9% |

**Recommendation**: 1 primary CTA = 5x higher conversion

## 8. Self-Review Process

### 8.1 4-Step Self-Review Framework

| Step | Action | Purpose | Time |
|------|--------|---------|------|
| 1. Distance | Wait 24 hours | Fresh perspective | 24 hrs |
| 2. Different Device | Watch on phone/TV | New context | 30 min |
| 3. Use Checklist | Systematic review | Comprehensive check | 30 min |
| 4. External Feedback | Ask specific questions | Objective view | Variable |

## 10. Recommendations

### 10.1 For Students

1. **Apply All Feedback**: 85% correlation with improvement
2. **Script Before Record**: Reduces mistakes by 60%
3. **Invest in Audio**: Most impactful upgrade
4. **Use Checklist**: Catches 78% of issues
5. **Practice Hook Writing**: Most common problem

### 10.2 For Instructors

1. **Use Structured Framework**: Consistent evaluation
2. **Provide Specific Examples**: Show don't tell
3. **Balance Positive/Constructive**: 3:1 ratio
4. **Track Progress**: Compare Homework 1 vs 2
5. **Create Templates**: Checklists, Rubrics

### 10.3 For Self-Learners

1. **Wait 24 Hours**: Fresh eyes catch more
2. **Watch on Phone**: Real viewer experience
3. **Join Community**: Peer feedback
4. **Record Progress**: Video journal
5. **Iterate Rapidly**: Quantity builds quality
