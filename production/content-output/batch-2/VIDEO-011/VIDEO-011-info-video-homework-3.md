## Design Notes

### Colors
- Primary: #2563EB (Blue)
- Pass/Success: #10B981 (Green)
- Fail/Warning: #EF4444 (Red)
- Accent: #F59E0B (Yellow)
- Background: #F3F4F6 (Light Gray)

### Icons to Use
- Checkmark for completed items
- X for items to avoid
- Dollar sign for revenue
- Chart for analytics
- Calendar for schedule
- Magnifying glass for audit

### Typography
- Headings: Bold sans-serif
- Body: Regular sans-serif
- Numbers: Extra bold
- Checklist: Monospace

### Visual Elements
- Progress bars for metrics
- Pie chart for revenue mix
- Checklist boxes
- Flow arrows for process
