## Table 8: Audio Problem Solutions

| Problem | Cause | Solution | Cost |
|---------|-------|----------|------|
| Echo/Reverb | Hard surfaces | Add soft materials (curtains, pillows) | Low |
| Background Noise | HVAC, street | Turn off AC, close windows | Free |
| Muffled Audio | Mic too far | Position mic 15-20cm from mouth | Free |
| Plosives (P, B sounds) | Direct airflow | Use pop filter or angle mic | 50-150 THB |
| Sibilance (S sounds) | Mic sensitivity | De-esser in post or position change | Free-Medium |
| Room Hum | Electronics | Unplug unnecessary devices | Free |
